Our Story

Ounje Cafe was founded by Edidiong when he started his Masters at Yale. It started in his little kitchen at the hostels in a bid to have some familarity after travelling across the world for Education. In the two states surrounding him, There were no other african restaurant and he saw an oppurtuninity to impact the lives of peopelle who were looking some kind of familarity.
